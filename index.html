About Me

Hello! I’m Love Lianne M. Domingo, a student who enjoys learning, leading, and creating. I have a deep interest in entrepreneurship and a passion for activities that allow me to express myself—especially baking, dancing, and singing.

My creativity and determination drive me to take on challenges and turn ideas into reality. I proudly served as the Supreme Pupil Government (SPG) President, where I developed strong leadership and communication skills by organizing events and representing student voices. Through my schoolwork, I’ve explored ethical entrepreneurship, teamwork, and the basics of business planning. I also enjoy designing projects that reflect who I am—like my own portfolio website, business concepts, and creative presentations.

Every experience helps me grow and become more confident. I dream of building a business that combines creativity and customer experience. With every project I create, I take one step closer to that goal, always learning along the way.
